Problem Discovery & Research

Many students in government schools lack awareness about proper personal hygiene practices, leading to health issues and absenteeism. Our research focused on understanding these challenges and identifying simple, impactful solutions.

Reduced absenteeism through hygiene awareness

50%

Interviews

We interacted with students and teachers to understand their daily hygiene habits, challenges, and awareness levels regarding personal cleanliness.

  • Students shared the importance of clean drinking water.
  • Teachers highlighted the need for better hygiene awareness.
  • Children responded positively to interactive learning activities.
  • Schools emphasized the importance of accessible hygiene facilities.

Problem Framing

The core issue identified was the lack of awareness and engaging hygiene education among students in government schools.

Ideation

We brainstormed creative ways to spread awareness, including visual reminders, interactive sessions, hygiene demonstrations, and activity-based learning.

Evaluation & Shortlisting

After evaluating multiple ideas, we selected practical and engaging approaches that would be easy for children to understand and remember.

Solution Framing

Our final solution focused on creating awareness through direct interaction, healthy habit reminders, and visual demonstrations for students.

Interactive Hygiene Session

Students participated in engaging awareness activities focused on healthy hygiene habits and cleanliness.

Healthy Habit Reminders

Children were encouraged to maintain habits like bathing regularly and eating healthy food every day.

Hygiene Awareness Stickers

We designed 12 illustrated hygiene stickers, each one capturing a core habit in a fun, child-friendly visual. Rather than just handing out informational sheets, we affixed the stickers directly onto the children's water bottles and bags during the visit. That way, the message travels home and becomes a daily visual prompt — a tiny nudge every time they reach for their bottle.

12 Illustrated Hygiene Stickers

The stickers were designed to encourage healthy habits through simple and memorable visual reminders that children could carry with them every day.
